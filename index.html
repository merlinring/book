Introduction

Introduction

Merlin Ring is an experimental project established on the Merlin Chain. We plan to create a PoW-based ERC20 and ERC720 token distribution protocol, establish a public Launch Pad, and use the funds raised to complete the production of Merlin Chain's first game, Merlin's Adventure.

The core of the Merlin Ring ecosystem is the Merlin Ring NFT. This is the first NFT based on the ERC721PoW protocol on Merlin Chain. Specifically, we innovatively use the PoW distribution mechanism to achieve fair distribution on the Merlin Chain for the first time. Merlin Ring is our ecosystem's genesis NFT. After the sale is completed, we will take snapshots at specific times based on the number of NFTs held and airdrop $MR to the holders.

$MR will serve as the official token of the Merlin Ring ecosystem, with no public sale rounds, and all tokens will be generated through airdrops. After the airdrop ends, the sales revenue of Merlin Ring will be used to add to the official liquidity pool and locked. The team's income depends on the token sales tax, which will motivate us to continuously deliver products. In the near future, $MR will serve as the official token of the game Merlin's Adventure and will be used for in-game item transactions.

Regarding the Launch Pad we will deliver in the future, what we can disclose is that the overall distribution mechanism will be based on PoW to ensure fairness. Similarly, holders of Merlin Ring and $MR can participate in staking to reduce the difficulty of participating in PoW, and specific details will be announced after the Launch Pad is delivered. Please see our Roadmap for more information.
