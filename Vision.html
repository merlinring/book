Vision

Vision

The ERC721PoW represents an innovative improvement in the distribution model of ERC721 assets, incorporating a Proof of Work (PoW) mechanism to determine the distribution and Minting eligibility for NFTs. This innovative method of asset distribution can significantly improve the existing distribution issues on the Merlin Chain.

We believe that the Merlin Chain holds great potential, but one apparent issue is the unfair distribution of assets. In previous projects that adopted direct sales on the blockchain, those with substantial funds and technical capabilities often dominated, resulting in only a small portion of the real assets falling into the hands of genuine collectors. This could quickly destroy an excellent project.

By innovatively integrating the Proof of Work mechanism into the distribution on the Merlin Chain, this distribution mechanism ensures that all participants have a fair chance to mint our tokens. The Proof of Work mechanism has been proven to be the fairest and most secure distribution mechanism. Under this system, everyone must compute the hash of a transaction to qualify for Minting. We place all verification mechanisms on the blockchain, ensuring all transactions are legal, transparent, and open.
